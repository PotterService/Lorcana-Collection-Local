Instructions

How to use Bow Lorcana Collector.

Your collection saves locally in your browser, not online.

Important

Export backups often so your collection is safe.

Nothing you save is stored online by this app. Owned counts, foil counts, wishlist marks, notes, and condition are saved locally in your browser.

Basic Use

  1. Open Home and click Pull Lorcana Sets.
  2. Open Sets and load the sets you want.
  3. Use + and − on cards to track owned and foil counts.
  4. Use Want Card to add cards to your Wishlist.
  5. Use My Collection to view only cards you own.
  6. Use Characters to browse character cards across loaded sets.

Backups

Use Settings to export a backup. If browser storage is cleared, your collection can be lost without a backup.

Saving Space

Settings can remove not-owned cards while keeping owned and wanted cards. You can redownload sets later.
