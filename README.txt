Bow Lorcana Collector v3

A magical storybook-style local Disney Lorcana collector app.

Pages:
- Home
- Sets
- Cards
- Set Detail
- My Collection
- Characters
- Wishlist
- Vault
- Settings
- Instructions
- Terms

How to use:
1. Open index.html through a local server or your hosted site.
2. Click Pull Lorcana Sets.
3. Open Sets and load the sets you want.
4. Track owned and foil counts.
5. Use My Collection, Characters, Wishlist, and Vault.

Storage:
- Uses IndexedDB for card and collection data.
- Your saves are local to the browser.
- Export backups from Settings.

Data:
- Pulls Lorcana sets and cards from Lorcast.
- Uses card image URLs returned by the card data.
- Prices show only when the card data includes price fields.
